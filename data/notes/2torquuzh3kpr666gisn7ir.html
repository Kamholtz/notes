  • r/AskElectronics - Looking for an IC/circuit that would allow me to safely charge a lipo battery that is still connected to a device (like a cellphone)

    • Anything that lists 1) powerpath, 2) Vsys output, or 3) uses an external current sense resistor should work fine - so BQ25606, CN3791, STNS01 (if your phone's current draw is unusually low), but not TP4056 or MCP738xx.

      • Cn3791 Solar Mppt Approx Charger (for solar with MPPT approximation)

      • Bq25606 Buck Lipo Battery Charger (not specifically for solar or MPPT like)

      • Stns01 Li Ion Linear Battery Charger with Ldo - Package and LDO make it unsuitable for my needs as the Nrf52840 has an inbuilt DC converter so regulating twice would be inefficient

      • Although Tp4056 and Mcp73837 Li Ion Battery Charger do not have an external current sense resistor, a power path can be created used the application not for the MCP73837 mentioned in Power Path using a P-channel MOSFET

    • The reason for these precautions is that lithum-ion batteries do not like being float-charged (held at 4.2v while current asymptotes to zero) as it causes them to grow dendrites and stab themselves to a fiery death.
